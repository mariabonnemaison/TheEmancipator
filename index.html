What is VoteChat?

VoteChat was created in partnership with Northeastern University to inform new voters on current and changing voting laws that either expand or suppress voter accessibility. Every state has different voting laws and requirement. These laws are often complicated of difficult to understand. These laws can be challenging for first time voters as they make voting seem like a difficult task. Insead, VoteChat has gathered all new laws from all 50 states and has translated them in speaking terms, so you know exactly what they mean and how they'll affect you. With the help of VoteChat New voters will also be able know what they need to prepare to vote when elections come around. Wether you plan to vote my mail or in person, VoteChat will help you let you know exactly what you need so you can vote confidently.
